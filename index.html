About us:

We are a team of specialists who have been working hard for more than a decade to solve all those damages that frequently affect the glass in our environment. whether residential or commercial, indoors or outdoors.


Impact glass panels maintenance

-Maintenance of impact glass panels.

- Stain removal on clear and tinted glass panels.

- Anti-static coating treatment specially designed to repel dust and water.

- Protection anti-rust and damage caused by UV

Shower Glass Restoration

Full restoration of glass showers. Elimination of scratches and stains caused by accumulation of calcium salts, hard water and detergents.

Professional services

Cars and boats

Residencial services

We restore and maintain all your glasses in your Residential environment, whether they are mirrors and glass tabletops.
